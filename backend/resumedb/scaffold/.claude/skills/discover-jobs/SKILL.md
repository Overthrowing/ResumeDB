---
name: discover-jobs
description: Discover and qualify jobs from a natural-language career goal.
---

Use the complete ResumeDB knowledge base to research live roles matching the
user's natural-language goal. Call POST /api/agent/command and let the universal
pipeline validate, deduplicate, and persist results. Rank fit from explicit hard
facts, semantic evidence, and preferences. Never infer missing factual profile
data. Medium-confidence roles stay in the discovery inbox, high-confidence
roles may prepare automatically through draft, and hard conflicts stay visible
with an explanation.
