## Intent

Open on the painful reality of internship hunting, then show how ResumeDB changes
the game for Sam Rivera. One persistent experience bank powers discovery,
qualification, evidence-backed tailoring, two dramatically different resumes,
application autofill, campaign tracking, and daily agent workflows.

This is a persuasive product pitch, not a technical tour. Every visual should
prove leverage: ResumeDB remembers more, searches faster, tailors more precisely,
and turns scattered applications into a focused career campaign.
